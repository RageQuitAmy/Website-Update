Down below are the Miner power-ups and a brief description on what they do. There are 13 in total.

- Pickaxe Efficiency I- Pickaxes dig 10% faster. The required level for this power-up is Miner level 5 and it costs 5 job coins.
- Pickaxe Efficiency II- Pickaxes dig 20% faster. The required level for this power-up is Miner level 15 and it costs 8 job coins.
- Pickaxe Efficiency III- Pickaxes dig 30% faster. The required level for this power-up is Miner level 30 and it costs 10 job coins.
- Pickaxe Efficiency IV- Pickaxes dig 40% faster. The required level for this power-up is Miner level 50 and it costs 12 job coins.
- Pickaxe Efficiency V- Pickaxes dig 50% faster. The required level for this power-up is Miner level 80 and it costs 15 job coins.

- Pickaxe Unbreaking I - Pickaxes are 10% less likely to take damage when used. The required level for this power-up is Miner level 5 and it costs 5 job coins.
- Pickaxe Unbreaking II - Pickaxes are 20% less likely to take damage when used. The required level for this power-up is Miner level 15 and it costs 8 job coins.
- Pickaxe Unbreaking III - Pickaxes are 30% less likely to take damage when used. The required level for this power-up is Miner level 30 and it costs 10 job coins.
- Pickaxe Unbreaking IV - Pickaxes are 40% less likely to take damage when used. The required level for this power-up is Miner level 50 and it costs 12 job coins.
- Pickaxe Unbreaking V - Pickaxes are 50% less likely to take damage when used. The required level for this power-up is Miner level 80 and it costs 15 job coins.

- Double Drops I - Grants a 5% chance to get double drops when mining blocks. The required level for this power-up is Miner level 10 and it costs 5 job coins.
- Double Drops II - Grants a 10% chance to get double drops when mining blocks. The required level for this power-up is Miner level 25 and it costs 10 job coins.
- Double Drops III - Grants a 20% chance to get double drops when mining blocks. The required level for this power-up is Miner level 50 and it costs 15 job coins.
