Down below are the Enchanter power-ups and a brief description on what they do. There are 6 in total.

- Job Experience I - Multiply your job experience gain by 1.5x. The required level for this power-up is Enchanter level 15 and it costs 5 job coins.
- Job Experience II - Multiply your job experience gain by 2x. The required level for this power-up is Enchanter level 25 and it costs 8 job coins.
- Job Experience III - Multiply your job experience gain by 3x. The required level for this power-up is Enchanter level 40 and it costs 12 job coins.

- Experience Boost I - Multiply your experience from blocks and mobs by 1.5x. The required level for this power-up is Enchanter level 15 and it costs 5 job coins.
- Experience Boost II - Multiply your experience from blocks and mobs by 2x. The required level for this power-up is Enchanter level 30 and it costs 8 job coins.
- Experience Boost III - Multiply your experience from blocks and mobs by 2.5x. The required level for this power-up is Enchanter level 50 and it costs 10 job coins.
