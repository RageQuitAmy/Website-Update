Down below are the Fisherman power-ups and a brief description on what they do. There are 6 in total.

- Grappling Hook I - Turns your fishing rod into a grappling hook that can be used to pull you to a location. The required level for this power-up is Fisherman level 10 and it costs 5 job coins.
- Grappling Hook II - Make your grappling hook pull you further. The required level for this power-up is Fisherman level 25 and it costs 8 job coins.
- Grappling Hook III - Make your grappling hook pull you even further. The required level for this power-up is Fisherman level 40 and it costs 10 job coins.

- Job Experience I - Multiply your job experience gain by 1.5x. The required level for this power-up is Fisherman level 15 and it costs 5 job coins.
- Job Experience II - Multiply your job experience gain by 2x. The required level for this power-up is Fisherman level 25 and it costs 8 job coins.
- Job Experience III - Multiply your job experience gain by 3x. The required level for this power-up is Fisherman level 40 and it costs 12 job coins.
