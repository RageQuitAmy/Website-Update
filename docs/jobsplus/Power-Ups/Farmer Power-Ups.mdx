Down below are the Farmer power-ups and a brief description on what they do. There are 6 in total.

- Double Drops I - Grants a 5% chance to get double drops when harvesting crops. The required level for this power-up is Farmer level 10 and it costs 5 job coins.
- Double Drops II - Grants a 10% chance to get double drops when harvesting crops. The required level for this power-up is Farmer level 25 and it costs 10 job coins.
- Double Drops III - Grants a 20% chance to get double drops when harvesting crops. The required level for this power-up is Farmer level 50 and it costs 15 job coins.

- Job Experience I - Multiply your job experience gain by 1.5x. The required level for this power-up is Farmer level 15 and it costs 5 job coins.
- Job Experience II - Multiply your job experience gain by 2x. The required level for this power-up is Farmer level 25 and it costs 8 job coins.
- Job Experience III - Multiply your job experience gain by 3x. The required level for this power-up is Farmer level 40 and it costs 12 job coins.
