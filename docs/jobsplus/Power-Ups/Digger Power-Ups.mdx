Down below are the Digger power-ups and a brief description on what they do. There are 18 in total.

- Gold Digger I - Grants a 1% chance to find a gold ingot when digging. The required level for this power-up is Digger level 5 and it costs 5 job coins.
- Gold Digger II - Grants a 3% chance to find a gold ingot when digging. The required level for this power-up is Digger level 15 and it costs 8 job coins.
- Gold Digger III - Grants a 5% chance to find a gold ingot when digging. The required level for this power-up is Digger level 30 and it costs 10 job coins.
- Gold Digger IV - Grants a 0.5% chance to find a gold block when digging. The required level for this power-up is Digger level 50 and it costs 12 job coins.
- Gold Digger V - Grants a 1% chance to find a gold block when digging. The required level for this power-up is Digger level 80 and it costs 15 job coins.

- Shovel Unbreaking I - Shovels are 10% less likely to take damage when used. The required level for this power-up is Digger level 5 and it costs 5 job coins.
- Shovel Unbreaking II - Shovels are 20% less likely to take damage when used. The required level for this power-up is Digger level 15 and it costs 8 job coins.
- Shovel Unbreaking III - Shovels are 30% less likely to take damage when used. The required level for this power-up is Digger level 30 and it costs 10 job coins.
- Shovel Unbreaking IV - Shovels are 40% less likely to take damage when used. The required level for this power-up is Digger level 50 and it costs 12 job coins.
- Shovel Unbreaking V - Shovels are 50% less likely to take damage when used. The required level for this power-up is Digger level 80 and it costs 15 job coins.

- Double Drops I - Grants a 5% chance to get double drops when digging. The required level for this power-up is Digger level 10 and it costs 5 job coins.
- Double Drops II - Grants a 10% chance to get double drops when digging. The required level for this power-up is Digger level 25 and it costs 10 job coins.
- Double Drops III - Grants a 20% chance to get double drops when digging. The required level for this power-up is Digger level 50 and it costs 15 job coins.

- Shovel Efficiency I - Shovels dig 10% faster. The required level for this power-up is Digger level 5 and it costs 5 job coins.
- Shovel Efficiency II - Shovels dig 20% faster. The required level for this power-up is Digger level 15 and it costs 8 job coins.
- Shovel Efficiency III - Shovels dig 30% faster. The required level for this power-up is Digger level 30 and it costs 10 job coins.
- Shovel Efficiency IV - Shovels dig 40% faster. The required level for this power-up is Digger level 50 and it costs 12 job coins.
- Shovel Efficiency V - Shovels dig 50% faster. The required level for this power-up is Digger level 80 and it costs 15 job coins.
