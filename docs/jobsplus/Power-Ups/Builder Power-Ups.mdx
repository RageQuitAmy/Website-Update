Down below are the Builder power-ups and a brief description on what they do. There are 3 in total.

- Less Fall Damage I - Take 20% less fall damage. The required level for this power-up is Builder level 15 and it costs 5 job coins.
- Less Fall Damage II - Take 40% less fall damage. The required level for this power-up is Builder level 30 and it costs 8 job coins.
- Less Fall Damage III - Take 60% less fall damage. The required level for this power-up is Builder level 50 it costs 10 job coins.
