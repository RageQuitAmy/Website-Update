Down below are the Lumberjack power-ups and a brief description on what they do. There are 13 in total.

- Axe Unbreaking I - Axes are 10% less likely to take damage when used. The required level for this power-up is Lumberjack level 5 and it costs 5 job coins.
- Axe Unbreaking II - Axes are 20% less likely to take damage when used. The required level for this power-up is Lumberjack level 15 and it costs 8 job coins.
- Axe Unbreaking III - Axes are 30% less likely to take damage when used. The required level for this power-up is Lumberjack level 30 and it costs 10 job coins.
- Axe Unbreaking IV - Axes are 40% less likely to take damage when used. The required level for this power-up is Lumberjack level 50 and it costs 12 job coins.
- Axe Unbreaking V - Axes are 50% less likely to take damage when used. The required level for this power-up is Lumberjack level 80 and it costs 15 job coins.

- Axe Efficiency I - Axes chop 10% faster. The required level for this power-up is Lumberjack level 5 and it costs 5 job coins.
- Axe Efficiency II - Axes chop 20% faster. The required level for this power-up is Lumberjack level 15 and it costs 8 job coins.
- Axe Efficiency III - Axes chop 30% faster. The required level for this power-up is Lumberjack level 30 and it costs 10 job coins.
- Axe Efficiency IV - Axes chop 40% faster. The required level for this power-up is Lumberjack level 50 and it costs 12 job coins.
- Axe Efficiency V - Axes chop 50% faster. The required level for this power-up is Lumberjack level 80 and it costs 15 job coins.

- Double Drops I - Grants a 5% chance to get double drops when harvesting wood. The required level for this power-up is Lumberjack level 10 and it costs 5 job coins.
- Double Drops II - Grants a 10% chance to get double drops when harvesting wood. The required level for this power-up is Lumberjack level 25 and it costs 10 job coins.
- Double Drops III - Grants a 20% chance to get double drops when harvesting wood. The required level for this power-up is Lumberjack level 50 and it costs 15 job coins.
