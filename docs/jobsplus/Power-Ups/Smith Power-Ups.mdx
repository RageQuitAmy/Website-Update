Down below are the Smith power-ups and a brief description on what they do. There are 8 in total.

- Armor Unbreaking I - Armor is 10% less likely to take damage when attacked. The required level for this power-up is Smith level 5 and it costs 5 job coins.
- Armor Unbreaking II - Armor is 20% less likely to take damage when attacked. The required level for this power-up is Smith level 15 and it costs 8 job coins.
- Armor Unbreaking III - Armor is 30% less likely to take damage when attacked. The required level for this power-up is Smith level 30 and it costs 10 job coins.
- Armor Unbreaking IV - Armor is 40% less likely to take damage when attacked. The required level for this power-up is Smith level 50 and it costs 12 job coins.
- Armor Unbreaking V - Armor is 50% less likely to take damage when attacked. The required level for this power-up is Smith level 80 and it costs 15 job coins.

- Job Experience I - Multiply your job experience gain by 1.5x. The required level for this power-up is Smith level 15 and it costs 5 job coins.
- Job Experience II - Multiply your job experience gain by 2x. The required level for this power-up is Smith level 25 and it costs 8 job coins.
- Job Experience III - Multiply your job experience gain by 3x. The required level for this power-up is Smith level 40 and it costs 12 job coins.
