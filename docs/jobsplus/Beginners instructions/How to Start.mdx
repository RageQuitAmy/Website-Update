To start your Jobs journey, follow the instructions listed below:

1. Press J (this will open the Jobs+ menu)
2. Select a job on the left hand side of the menu and click start performing this job. You can do this twice as you get 2 free jobs to begin with. After this, each job costs 10 coins.

Once you have started a job, you can start earning job exp by doing job tasks.
